🎮 Bagh-Chal (Tiger and Goat Game)

The main objective for the tigers is to hunt (capture) the goats, whereas the goats try to trap and block the tigers so they cannot move. The unique dynamics and roles in the game make it a challenging and engaging test of foresight and planning.

Though native to Nepal, a similar version is also seen in southern India, with minor board variations but identical rules. The game remains popular, especially in rural areas, where it's not only a pastime but also a cultural symbol of strategic thinking.
